About Ellen

Ellen Heunisch is a fourth-year undergraduate at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire. She majors in Integrated Strategic Communications with an emphasis in Advertising and PR. She also has minors in History and Multimedia Communications.

At UWEC, Ellen has gained diverse hands-on experience serving as the Director of Publicity and Promotion and emcee for GEEKcon 2024. Ellen also supports fellow students as a peer advisor for first-generation college students in the Student Support Services (SSS) program. During Ellen's sophomore year, she also took on the role of a Resident Assistant (RA) for 32 incoming freshmen, study abroad students, and first-generation individuals. Ellen participated in the UWEC study abroad program during spring 2024 in Winchester, England, to finish her History minor. Ellen also conducted international field research with the UWEC Geography department using ground penetrating radar (GPR) to locate Holocaust mass graves in Latvia and Lithuania.

Ellen will be graduating in May 2026 and is currently looking for her next challenge as she enters her next stage in life.
